⚡ How to Play

🎮
Controls

←→ or AD to move · Space / ↑ to jump · ↑↓ to climb ladders

⭐
Goal

Reach the glowing star before time runs out. Touch platforms to understand each physics force.

❤️
Lives & Danger

You have 3 lives. Hazards, falling off screen, or time-up each cost 1 life.

🔥
Difficulty

Platforms shrink, hazards multiply, time shrinks — every 3 levels you'll feel the heat.

⚡
Speed Bonus

Complete a level fast for a time bonus! Combo fast levels for a score multiplier.

📱
Mobile

Touch the on-screen buttons to move & jump. Works great on phones and tablets!
